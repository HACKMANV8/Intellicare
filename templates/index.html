How It Works

1. Scan QR Code

Use our app to scan the QR code on your IntelliCare gloves to pair them with your device.

2. Wear the Gloves

3. Monitor Data

About IntelliCare

IntelliCare is a revolutionary health monitoring system that combines wearable technology with advanced sensors to provide real-time health data.

Our smart gloves are equipped with multiple sensors to monitor oxygen levels, blood pressure, heart rate, and movement patterns, providing comprehensive health insights.

Designed for both healthcare professionals and individuals, IntelliCare aims to make health monitoring accessible, accurate, and convenient.
